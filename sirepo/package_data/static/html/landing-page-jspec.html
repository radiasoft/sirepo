JSPEC

JSPEC is an open source C++ package for numerical simulations on the electron cooling process, including the intrabeam scattering (IBS) effect, developed at Jefferson Lab (JLab).

The Sirepo GUI is primarily supported by the development team at RadiaSoft LLC.
Documentation
  • Full source code is available from GitHub.
  • A detailed JSPEC User Manual is provided.
Sirepo and Jupyter server development has been supported by the U.S. Department of Energy Office of Science, under multiple awards. Work with JSPEC is supported by the Office of Nuclear Physics under Award No. DE-SC0015212.
